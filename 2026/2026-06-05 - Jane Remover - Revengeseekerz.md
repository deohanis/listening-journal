## General thoughts
Revengeseekerz is the third studio album by the American musician Jane Remover, as the follow up to their second studio album, Census Designated. Following two earlier scrapped attempts at a third studio album, it was conceptualized while touring with JPEGMafia on his Lay Down My Life Tour and recorded shortly afterwards, concurrently with the album Ghostholding on their side project Venturing. The album draws heavily from Remover's experiences with newfound fame, exploring how it impacted their mental health and relationships. In contrast to the more polished sound of their previous studio album, Revengeseekerz revisits the digicore style of Frailty while incorporating elements of hip-hop, hyperpop, and house music. As with their previous two albums, production was handled entirely by Jane Remover. Sonically, the album is characterized by its dense layering of sound effects and samples, many of which reference Remover's earlier work, creating a self-reflexive throughline across the track list.
