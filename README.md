# Listening Journal

My personal music diary. Every LP, EP, single, and project I listen to gets a full write-up with track-by-track notes, ratings, and thoughts.

Final reviews are also made available on my <a href="https://www.albumoftheyear.org/user/deohanis/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">AOTY.</a>
---

## Rating System

A simple yet effective rating system with increments of ten (10).

| Score | Label                          | Meaning |
|-------|--------------------------------|---------|
| **100** | Masterpiece                   | Flawless. Instant classic. Will be in my all-time rotation forever. |
| **90**  | Near Perfection               | Exceptional. Tiny flaws at most. |
| **80**  | Strong Direction, Minor Flaws | Really good. Minor issues keep it from being elite. |
| **70**  | Good, Had Potential but Held Back by Issues | Solid but held back by clear problems. |
| **60**  | Solid Project                 | Enjoyable, no major complaints, but nothing transcendent. |
| **50**  | Middle of the Road            | Average. Nothing special, nothing terrible. |
| **40**  | Below Average                 | Noticeably weak. Skippable. |
| **30**  | Poor Overall                  | Bad. Struggled to finish. |
| **20**  | Deeply Flawed                 | Painful. Major issues everywhere. |
| **10**  | HANS GET THE LUGER!!!         | One of the worst things I’ve ever heard. |

## How This Journal Is Organized

- **Year folders** (`2026/`, `2025/`, etc.) → All entries are stored by listen date.
- **File naming convention**: `YYYY-MM-DD - Artist - Title.md`
- Every entry uses the same template (see `Templates/listen-template.md`)
- Full track-by-track notes, General thoughts, Final Reviews, etc.
